>The tech industry embraced that kind of progress by turning it into a narrative of quality for commercial products and branded it “beta.”
<br /><br />
Because there’s always an update waiting round the corner, we’ve all begun to salivate in anticipation of the next model. For innovation it’s brilliant. But when dealing with democratic institutions and its problems, it promotes laziness.**<a href="https://mayday.co/let-them-eat-cake">Let them eat cake</a>**
