>The key to the product needs hierarchy is one that Walter only mentions tangentially: your product has to satisfy the needs at each level before moving on to the next. A product can’t be efficient if it isn’t functional. A product can’t be delightful if it isn’t efficient and functional.**<a href="https://matthewstrom.com/writing/delight-comes-last/">Delight comes last</a>**

It's a <a href="https://matthewstrom.com/writing/delight-comes-last/">great post by Matthew Ström</a>, one of my favorite writers. I wrote about the <a href="/great-ux/">same topic back in 2015</a> but didn't address the above point at all.
